Research positions and education

Research Fellowship, Jesus College, University of Cambridge (2014-2019)

  • Independent academic position, providing three years’ funding and accommodation to research in any branch of the sciences.
  • Selected for one position from 87 applicants and believed to be the first ever Research Fellow in any branch of chemistry.
  • Option of intermission to research elsewhere.

Postdoctoral Associate, Cornell University, USA (Sep 2015- Jul 2017)

  • Intermitted Research Fellowship to undertake research at Cornell in the group of Prof Nandini Ananth.
  • Researched non-adiabatic dynamics, singlet fission and chromophore design.

PhD (Theoretical Chemistry), Trinity College, University of Cambridge (2011-2014)

  • Supervised by Prof. Stuart Althorpe
  • Derived a unique Quantum Transition-State Theory which had been claimed not to exist since 1939.
  • Dissertation on arxiv, and available as PDF.

MChem (Chemistry) 1st Class, Exeter College, University of Oxford (2007-2011)

  • Highest overall mark in the University of Oxford.
  • Fourth year research project supervised by Prof. David Manolopoulos FRS, developing non-adiabatic Ring-Polymer Molecular Dynamics. Thesis on arxiv.
  • Highest mark in University for 3rd year (Part IB) exams.
  • Highest mark in University for 2nd year (Part IA) exams.
  • Fifth equal (out of 197) in University for 1st year (Preliminary) exams.
  • Supplementary subjects taken: History and Philosophy of Science (2011), Distinction; Quantum Chemistry (2010), Distinction; Aromatic and Heterocyclic Chemistry (2009), Distinction.
